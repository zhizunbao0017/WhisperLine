Privacy Policy · WhisperLine

Last Updated: November 11, 2025

WhisperLine is a personal diary designed to keep your reflections private. This policy explains what data stays on your device, when information leaves the device, and how permissions are used.

Core Principle: Your Data is Yours

We do not run cloud servers for WhisperLine. Diary content, mood analytics, settings, and images remain on the iOS device you use. We never collect, sell, or profile your information.

1. Information Stored Locally

  • Diary entries & attachments: Text, images, moods, and weather notes are stored in iOS local storage (AsyncStorage and the app documents directory).
  • Mood insights: Sentiment scores and wellness suggestions are generated on-device and saved with the related entry so Insights can visualize trends offline.
  • App preferences: Theme choice, avatar selection, and lock-state are recorded locally.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require an account, and we do not collect your name or email address.
  • We never upload diary text, images, moods, or analytics to our servers.
  • We do not log precise location history or background location data.

3. Optional Third-Party Requests

WhisperLine can fetch local weather so you can tag entries with the day's conditions. When you tap "Add Current Weather," iOS asks for permission to use approximate location. If you agree:

  • Coordinates are sent directly from your device to the OpenWeatherMap service ( privacy policy ) solely for that request.
  • WhisperLine stores only the returned city, temperature (°C), and conditions string. Coordinates are not saved.

4. Permissions Explained

  • Photos: Needed to insert pictures from your library. Images stay within the app's documents directory.
  • Location: Optional, used only when you request weather.

5. App Lock and Security

Enabling App Lock uses Apple's Face ID or Touch ID APIs. WhisperLine never sees or stores your biometric data; the check happens in iOS. While data is kept locally, it is stored in the standard iOS app sandbox. Please ensure your device passcode is enabled for additional protection.

6. Children's Privacy

WhisperLine is not directed to children under 13 and should be used by adults or with adult supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
